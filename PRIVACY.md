# Health Sync Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Health Sync helps you export selected Apple Health data to your own iCloud Drive as machine-readable JSON files.

## Information the app accesses

Health Sync asks permission to read supported Apple Health data types, including sleep and other categories you choose to export. Health data is read only after you grant HealthKit permission in iOS.

Health Sync does not write data back to Apple Health.

## Where data goes

Exported files are written to the app-defined Health Sync Export folder in your iCloud Drive. This uses Apple iCloud infrastructure associated with your Apple ID. The developer does not operate a separate server for these exports and does not receive the exported health files.

## Information the developer collects

Health Sync does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, third-party crash reporting SDKs, or cross-app tracking. The app does not require an account.

The developer may receive information only if you choose to contact support, for example through a GitHub issue. In that case, the information you provide is used to respond to your request.

## Purchases

Health Sync uses Apple StoreKit for optional subscription purchase status. Apple processes purchases and may provide subscription entitlement status to the app so automatic background export cadence can be enabled for subscribers. Manual export and HealthKit access remain free.

## Data retention and deletion

Health export files remain under your control in iCloud Drive. You can delete exported files from iCloud Drive at any time. You can revoke Health permissions in iOS Settings. You can delete the app to remove its local app data from your device according to iOS behavior.

Support requests submitted through GitHub remain subject to GitHub's retention and privacy practices. Do not include sensitive health data in support requests.

## Children

Health Sync is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

## Contact

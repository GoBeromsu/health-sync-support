# Health Sync Support

Health Sync exports selected Apple Health records to iCloud Drive as machine-readable JSON files.

## Get help

Open an issue here:

https://github.com/GoBeromsu/health-sync-support/issues

Do not include personal health data, screenshots containing health records, or private Apple ID information in public support requests.

## Common checks

- Confirm iCloud Drive is enabled on the device.
- Confirm Health permission is granted in iOS Settings.
- Check Files > iCloud Drive > Health Sync Export for exported files.
- Manual export and HealthKit access are free. The optional Pro subscription only gates automatic background export cadence.
